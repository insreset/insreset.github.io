The Russian Language

Hello! This server is focused on building a friendly and open community, where we study Russian together. We believe that everyone should have access to knowledge, and that it should be shared with others.

We regularly host Russian classes. They are free, and anybody can join. We invite everyone who would like to learn Russian to join our language learning server. The only rules are to be friendly and respectful.
